KickMap
KickMap serves as an on-campus event organizer and digital billboard tailored for students, enabling easy discovery, promotion, and management of campus activities. Students can browse events by location, time, or category, while organizers post announcements with maps and RSVPs. The platform fosters campus engagement through a centralized, user-friendly interface.​

Features
Event Discovery: Interactive campus map with pinned events, filters for categories (clubs, sports, academics), and search functionality.

Organizer Tools: Simple event creation with descriptions, images, dates, locations, and RSVP tracking.

Student Engagement: Push notifications, calendar integration, and social sharing for upcoming events.

Real-time Updates: Live billboard-style feed of featured events and announcements.

Campus Navigation: Integrated maps for finding event venues across campus buildings and outdoor spaces.
